About

I am a lecturer in Biological Sciences at the School of Environmental and Life Sciences, University of Hull, and Programme Director of the MSc Environmental Change, Management and Monitoring.

My research focuses on spatial ecology in disturbed landscapes, exploring how species adapt their behaviour in response to environmental change to support long-term conservation strategies.

I am particularly interested in the application of GIS and remote sensing to biodiversity conservation, and I collaborate across multidisciplinary teams including academia and industry.

Member of the British Ecological Society and the IUCN Connectivity Conservation Specialist Group.
